Hello World!

Visualisation of warm water intrusion on the Antartic Continental shelf via a canyon, with data from an idealised isopycnal model. Surface shown is the top density surface of warm water, colours overlayed show speed in the warm water layer.

There is a fast flowing Antarctic Slope Current off the continental shelf, and a cycle of warm water flooding onto and leaking off the continental shelf. This variability is intrinsic to the slope current system.
